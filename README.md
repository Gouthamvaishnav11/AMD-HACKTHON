# Hackathon Themes & Problem Statements

Welcome to the hackathon! Below are the key themes and the problems you can focus on while building your projects.

---

## 1. Generative AI for Everyone
**Essence:** Open up creation—text, image, audio, video, and code—to any student, club, or campus team.  

**Problem:**  
Many students and campus teams have **creative ideas** but **lack time, technical skills, or resources** to use AI effectively. Existing tools are often too complex, costly, or domain-specific.  

**Key Pain Points:**  
- Non-CS students struggle to use AI tools.  
- Limited time and budget for projects or events.  
- Collaboration challenges across team roles.  
- Responsible AI considerations (bias, attribution, content moderation).  
- Device/resource limitations for on-campus or offline use.  

**Hackathon Challenge:**  
Design AI tools that **democratize creativity**, are **easy to use**, **collaborative**, **efficient**, and **responsible**.

---

## 2. AI in Education & Skilling
**Essence:** Personalize learning with explainable, multilingual support that boosts confidence and outcomes.  

**Problem:**  
Students have **diverse learning styles**, but current educational tools provide **one-size-fits-all guidance**, causing confusion, anxiety, and slower mastery.  

**Key Pain Points:**  
- Lack of adaptive, personalized learning.  
- Limited stepwise or rubric-based feedback.  
- Language barriers and missing multilingual support.  
- Inefficient study planning and progress tracking.  
- Academic integrity: plagiarism and cheating concerns.  

**Hackathon Challenge:**  
Create AI-powered educational tools that **personalize learning**, **offer explainable feedback**, **support multilingual users**, and **improve mastery and confidence**.

---

## 3. AI in Consumer Experiences
**Essence:** Personalized, delightful interactions in retail, media, travel, and campus services.  

**Problem:**  
Consumers and campus users often face **impersonal, confusing, or opaque systems**, reducing engagement, trust, and satisfaction.  

**Key Pain Points:**  
- Lack of transparency in recommendations.  
- Limited personalization for budgets, schedules, and preferences.  
- Fragmented planning across travel, events, or daily activities.  
- Challenges in producing consistent, on-brand media for clubs and teams.  
- Filter bubbles limit discovery and diversity.  

**Hackathon Challenge:**  
Build AI-powered consumer experiences that **personalize interactions**, **explain recommendations**, **respect constraints**, and **enhance discovery and trust**.

---

## 4. Open Innovation
**Essence:** Bring bold ideas that don’t fit neatly into other categories—surprise us with originality and impact.  

**Problem:**  
Breakthroughs often emerge **outside established categories**, but many projects stick to familiar domains, limiting creativity and societal impact.  

**Key Pain Points:**  
- Conventional categories constrain innovation.  
- Need for novel agents, modalities, or human–AI interaction patterns.  
- Low-resource or edge-first approaches are underexplored.  
- Combining domains (e.g., sustainability + education) is rare.  
- Ethical, measurable outcomes are often missing from day one.  

**Hackathon Challenge:**  
Develop **original, high-impact AI solutions** that explore new modalities, domains, or approaches while ensuring **user value, ethics, and feasibility**.
